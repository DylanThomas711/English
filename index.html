One of Troys goals is to make sure that he is the provider. He wants to make sure he can give his family financial stability, and make sure they can do the same for their future families.

Troys dream came true in terms of Cory. After refusing to send him to college for football where Troy believed Cory would be crushed and rejected, Cory decided to persue a very different career, that still used similar skills to the ones he would have used in football, joining the marines. This field provided him with a stable and honorable income so that when he does have a family he will be able to provide as well if not better than his father was able to.

His dream did not come true when it came to Lyons. Troys was not there for Lyons childhood, so it would be harder to convince him to take a stable and "safe" path in life rather than music. Because of this, Lyons never learned what Cory did, and eventually ended up in jail.
